Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my phone data to the web dashboard?

Simply log in to the app on your phone. Your data will automatically sync to the cloud when you're authenticated. You can then access the same data on the web dashboard by logging in with the same account.

How do I add an RFP using voice?

Go to the RFPs tab and tap the large microphone button. Speak your RFP details using keywords like "Case", "Broker", "Premium", "Lives", "Effective Date", and "Notes". The app will automatically parse and fill in the fields for you to review before saving.

How does the Sales Goal Tracker work?

The Sales Goal Tracker on the Home screen shows your progress toward your annual sales target. When you move an RFP to "Sold" status, its premium is automatically added to your current sales total. You can edit your goal amount and deadline by tapping the pencil icon.

Can I use the AI assistant to manage my schedule?

Yes! Go to the Chat tab and tell the AI what you need. You can say things like "Add a meeting with John tomorrow at 2pm" or "What's on my schedule this week?" The AI can create events, set reminders, and give you an overview of your commitments.

How do I export my data?

On the Home screen, scroll down to the Export section. You can generate a PDF Attack Plan (daily summary), export your RFPs to Excel, or export your schedule to Excel.

How do I delete my account?

To request account deletion and removal of all associated data, please email support@salesedge.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
