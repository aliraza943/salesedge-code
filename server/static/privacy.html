Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

SalesEdge ("we", "our", or "the App") is a sales management and productivity application designed for insurance sales professionals. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use SalesEdge, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your name and email address through our authentication provider to identify your account.
  • Sales Data: RFPs, broker contacts, calendar events, deals, sales goals, and chat messages that you create within the App. This data is stored to enable cloud sync across your devices.
  • Voice Input: When you use the voice dictation feature, audio is temporarily processed to transcribe your speech into text. Audio recordings are not stored permanently.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers to support local data storage and sync functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (data management, cloud sync, AI assistant)
  • Synchronize your data between the mobile app and web dashboard
  • Process voice input for RFP creation and chat interactions
  • Generate reports and exports (Attack Plans, Excel exports) at your request

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in cloud databases with encryption in transit. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Local data is stored on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your sales data, broker information, and other business data remain private to your account. We may use third-party services (authentication, AI processing) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual records (RFPs, brokers, events, deals) at any time through the App. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us at the support email below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view all data stored in your account
  • Edit or delete any records you have created
  • Export your data (via the App's export features)
  • Request complete deletion of your account and data

7. Children's Privacy

SalesEdge is designed for professional use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
